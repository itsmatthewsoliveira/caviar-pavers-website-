Your pool is the centerpiece of your backyard—the deck should match. We design and install pool decks that are as beautiful as they are functional.

  • Cool-Touch Materials – Comfortable even in peak summer heat
  • Slip-Resistant Surfaces – Safety for wet conditions

Materials Made for Water

Pool and waterfront environments demand special materials. We specialize in surfaces that perform beautifully in wet conditions.

  • Travertine – Natural stone, stays cool, excellent grip when wet
  • Brick Pavers – Classic look with textured surfaces
  • Concrete Pavers – Cool-touch technology options
  • Porcelain – Ultra-modern, impervious to water and staining

Precision Installation

Pool deck installation requires special expertise. Water, drainage, and existing pool structures create unique challenges we've mastered.

  • Slope Engineering – Proper grades direct water away
  • Coping Integration – Seamless transition from pool to deck
  • Expansion Joints – Properly placed to prevent cracking
  • Equipment Access – Maintain access to pool equipment
