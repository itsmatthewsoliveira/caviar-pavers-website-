## IMAGE SPECIFICATIONS

### Dimensions
- **Hero Images**: 1920x1080px (16:9 ratio)
- **Section Images**: 1400x1000px or 700x500px
- **Gallery Images**: 800x600px
- **Material Samples**: 400x300px

### Style Guidelines
- **Lighting**: Golden hour preferred (warm, sunset tones)
- **Location**: Florida-style homes with palm trees
- **Quality**: Professional real estate/architecture photography style
- **Colors**: Complement brand colors (Navy #1C263F, Gold #FFBC0D)
- **Mood**: Luxury, premium, aspirational

### Drone Footage Notes
For hero sections marked "DRONE FOOTAGE LOCATION", you can use your drone footage here. Recommended shots:
1. Aerial flyover of completed projects
2. Slow descending shot revealing full property
3. Orbit around pool/patio area
4. Sunrise/sunset golden hour timing
