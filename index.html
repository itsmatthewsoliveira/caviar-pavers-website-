Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical paver project take?

Most residential paver projects take 3-7 days depending on size and complexity. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we partner with trusted lenders to offer flexible financing plans that fit your budget. Ask us about 0% APR options.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve Jacksonville and all of Northeast Florida, including St. Augustine, Ponte Vedra, Orange Park, and surrounding areas.

Do you handle permits?

Absolutely. We handle all necessary permits and ensure your project meets local building codes and HOA requirements.

What warranty do you provide?

We offer a 5-year workmanship warranty on all installations, plus manufacturer warranties on materials ranging from 15-25 years.

Can I see examples of your work?

Of course! Visit our portfolio page or request references from recent projects in your neighborhood.
