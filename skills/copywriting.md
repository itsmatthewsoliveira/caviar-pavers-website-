# Copywriting Guidelines

## Tone of Voice
- **Upright & Professional:** "Experts elevating lives", "Commitment to excellence".
- **Elegant:** "Timeless charm", "Bespoke solutions".

## Service Categories (Strict Segmentation)
### 1. Hardscaping (The Core)
- **Patios:** "Extension of your living space."
- **Pool Decks:** "Safety meets luxury."
- **Walkways:** "Welcoming pathways."
- **Paver Driveways:** "Grand entrances."

### 2. Outdoor Living & Features
- **Artificial Turf:** "Lush, low-maintenance beauty."
- **Screen Enclosures:** "Comfort without compromise."
- **Pergolas/Gazebos:** "Architectural shade."

## Heading Styles (Luzen Style)
- **Hero:** ELEGANCE MEETS DURABILITY.
- **Section 01:** DISCOVER OUR EXPERTISE.
- **Section 02:** DESIGN WITH VISION.

## Lead Capture Form Copy
- **Header:** "Start Your Transformation" or "Get Your Free Quote".
- **Fields:** Name, Phone, Email, Project Type (Dropdown).
- **Button:** "REQUEST CONSULTATION".
