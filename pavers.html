02

The Caviar Difference

What separates a good paver job from a great one? It's all in the details.

Proper Sand Base

We use the right amount of quality sand, properly graded and compacted. This creates the stable foundation your pavers need.

Perfect Leveling

Every paver is set with precision. Proper slope for drainage while maintaining a perfectly flat surface. No wobbles.

Tight Joints

Pavers that don't shift or separate. Our installation technique and polymeric sand lock everything in place permanently.

Proper Drainage

Water is the enemy. We engineer every project with proper grading to direct water away from your home.

Solid Borders

Edge restraints are critical. We install heavy-duty borders that keep your pavers locked in position forever.

Clean Finish

We don't leave until every joint is filled, every edge is perfect, and your property is cleaner than when we arrived.
